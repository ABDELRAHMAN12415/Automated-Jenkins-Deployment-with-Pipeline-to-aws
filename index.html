About the Scholarship

The DEPI Scholarship Track aims to support aspiring DevOps engineers by providing them with the resources, mentorship, and education needed to succeed in the field.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be pursuing a degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Strong interest in DevOps practices and tools.
  • Demonstrated leadership skills and community involvement.

Application Process

To apply for the DEPI Scholarship, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit your resume and cover letter.
  3. Provide two letters of recommendation.
